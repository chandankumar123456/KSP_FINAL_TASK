Rolls-Royce BoatTail :

The Rolls-Royce Boat Tail is a nautical dream on wheels. Limited to three bespoke beauties, each boasts a hand-built aluminum body inspired by J-class yachts and a "butterfly-deck" that unfolds like a luxurious oasis. Inside, expect bespoke opulence with rotating cocktail tables, champagne-matching fridges, and a hidden parasol for alfresco sipping. Powered by a V12 beast, it's a $28 million masterpiece that whispers "yacht life" on every curve.

Boat Tail Price: Your yacht money just became pocket change.

Rolls-Royce DropTail :

The Rolls-Royce Droptail, unlike its yacht-inspired cousin the Boat Tail, is a sleek two-seater roadster, a modern take on classic coachbuilt elegance. It offers a higher degree of customization within its platform, with hand-sculpted beauty, minimalist luxury, and a roaring V12. While not quite the $28 million Boat Tail, it still demands respect on the road, a wind-in-your-hair expression of bespoke luxury for the ultra-wealthy.

Droptail Price: Not quite a Cullinan Black Badge, but still enough to make your accountant faint.
